Show a sequence of six images of scene `CBspheres.dae` rendered with `max_ray_depth` set to 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 100. The other settings should be at least 64 samples per pixel and 4 samples per light. Make sure to include all screenshots.








Point out the new multibounce effects that appear in each image. Explain how these bounce numbers relate to the particular effects that appear. Make sure to include all screenshots.

With max ray depth set to 0, nothing but the light source is visible after zero bounces. Only the light is visible because the light does not bounce.
With max ray depth set to 1, the floor, the walls, the light source, and the light source reflection off the spheres are now visible after one bounce. he right sphere is also refractive, so the light patch is has more spots than the left sphere which is only reflective. The light bounces off the floor, walls, and spheres into the camera.
With max ray depth set to 2, everything at max ray depth 1 is visible along with the cieling and also the walls, floor, and light source reflected clearly in the left sphere and less so in the right sphere due to refraction. The light now bounces off the cieling into the camera after two bounces. The light bounces off the walls, floor, and spheres onto the other spheres and into the camera.
With max ray depth set to 3, everything at max ray depth 2 is visible along with the reflection of the cieling and the walls in both spheres as well as the the circle of light under the glass sphere on the floor off of the right sphere. The light bounces three times: (1) off the floors/walls onto (2) the cieling/spheres onto (3) the spheres into the camera and/or onto the circle of light off the right sphere into the camera.
With max ray depth set to 4, everything at max ray depth 3 is visible along with the reflection of the right sphere in the left sphere and the reflection of light on the blue wall. The light bounces four times: (1) off the floor/walls onto (2) the cieling/spheres refracting/reflecting onto (3) the spheres onto (4) the blue wall and left sphere off the right sphere and into the camera.
With max ray depth set to 5, everything at max ray depth 4 is visible and overall global illumination is higher. The light bounces five times: (1) off the floor/walls onto (2) the cieling/spheres refracting/reflecting onto (3) the spheres onto (4) the blue wall and left sphere off the right sphere onto (5) the walls/floor, spheres, reflecting and refracting onto circle of light into the camera.
With max ray depth set to 100, everything at max ray depth 5 is visible and overall global illumination is higher, but there are no new significant multibounce effects between 5 and 100.
